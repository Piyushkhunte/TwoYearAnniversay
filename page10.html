Across oceans and time,
love always finds its way home.

Nine months apart, miles of salt and sea between us—
yet your voice echoed louder than the waves.
Every sunrise on deck felt softer
knowing somewhere, you were watching the same sky.

You waited through storms I could never describe,
through silent nights and slow messages,
and still, you loved me like I never left.
That’s how I knew distance had no power over us.

We’ve learned that love isn’t measured in time or touch,
but in the unspoken promise — I’ll always come back to you.

You are my harbor, my calm after every storm.

Happy 2 Years, my anchor ⚓
